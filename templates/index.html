Welcome to Binary Triage

Yowassup! Welcome to my little space on the net where I get to talk about myself and what I do (Yay!).

This place exists as an expression of myself to build something tangible from the ground up. It's built as a tribute and appreciation of knowledge, life and especially programming! This site is written in Python using Flask upon the Gunicorn webserver. Python isn't my language of choice for scripting websites (my choice would be Perl), but that's exactly why I'm writing this site in python!

Let me tell you a little bit about myself. When I was in high school, I wasn't really preoccupied with my grades or coursework. I struggled a lot trying to convince myself of how to fit in with the world. My response to this complex adventure was as childish as you could expect for a teenager: To retreat to the fantasy world of video games, where I could better my skills in a world where I was the hero and appreciated. This happened when I was about 10 years old, and after a few years of having my imagination captured, I discovered the world of MMORPG's through Ragnarok Online. It was around this point where I felt a lot more secure within myself as I had an online community that appreciated me for what I could do, and I worked hard to earn that respect and appreciation. There was a complex and arduous exploration of individuality between there and my uni life which included flunking the HSC and doing a TAFE course in Game Design.

After my diploma, what I learnt did not cut it for me. I was frustrated with what I learnt and with myself for getting into this situation, and I made the decision at TAFE to go to university and learn something the proper way. At this point, I was an expert user of windows (I knew how the internet worked from a young age. I was a gnarly net surfer and avid user of Macromedia(Adobe) Flash, Photoshop and even hosted my own Ragnarok Online server which as you'd expect of a 12 year old at the time, was hosted on 'some kids desktop in his parents basement'. I had 5 users on my server tops.

Fast forward a couple of years, and I am a tutor of Algorithms and Data at the University of New South Wales. Score.
